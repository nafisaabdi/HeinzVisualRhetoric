About Our Henna Store


Tradition since 2003

The Henna Store was founded in the early 2000's by Jamila. Her family migrated to Minnesota after fleeing the civil war in Somalia. Her family was able to thrive within the community using her entrepreneurial mindset. The dedication and time it takes to ensure quality within all the products is what Jamila stands for. The products used on clients are sourced from the best of ingredients that have undergone thurow testing. Additionally, every customer undergoes an allergy test to ensure their health is always placed at the highest priority. Take a chance on us and we'll surely become your go to Henna spot!

Karmel Mall Location

Jamila's Henna Store is located within the Karmel Mall. It is a Somali Mall full of culture and a hub of activity. The mall houses hundreds of stores and caters to the Minnesota Somali population. You'll find us on the third level of the mall in room 308. Stop by and enjoy the culture everyone's welcome!
