Heinz Brand

Heinz has been able to build a brand that capitalizes off of quality. Henry John Heinz was of the strong belief that “to do a common thing uncommonly well brings success.” This is clearly seen in the many successful ad campaigns intended to highlight the quality of the product. It is also seen clearly within their slogan.

Heinz is grown not made.

The fact that Heinz created Ketchup undeniably plays a huge role in their success. However, the distinction that the product is grown and not made is of high importance. This distinction has allowed them to become a household name and beat out other brands. Most notable is their ability to change based on customer needs. They have been able to keep up to date by offering customers variety. Meanwhile they still ensure that same classic taste that customers have come to rely on. Heinz has built a brand that is able to showcase their core values and assure the conscience consumer to keep coming back. Key Brand Values Consumer First Quality Innovation Integrity Ownership
